THE SOLUTION

Managed Entitlements

Subscribe to a Bedrock model once. Distribute the license to every account in your org through AWS License Manager. No repeat subscriptions needed.

Centralized management
One subscription, one license. See all model access from a single pane in License Manager.
Org-wide distribution
Grant to your entire Organization ID. New accounts automatically inherit access.
Compliance-friendly
Clear audit trail of who has access, when it was granted, and by whom.
Revoke anytime
Delete a grant to instantly remove access. Fine-grained control per account or org-wide.

DISTRIBUTION

Two ways to share access

Grant to Organization ID

One grant covers all member accounts

New accounts auto-inherit

Bulk activate from management account

Best for: broad access across the org

Grant to individual accounts

Target specific account IDs

Recipient must accept + activate

New accounts need manual grants

Best for: controlled rollout, specific teams

FAQ

Common questions

Is a subscription per model?
Yes. Each model requires its own Marketplace subscription. Each subscription creates a separate license you can distribute independently.
What about new accounts?
Org-wide grants auto-cover new member accounts. Individual grants require manual creation for each new account.
Can I revoke access?
Yes. Delete the grant anytime - the recipient immediately loses access to invoke the model.
How long does distribution take?
Minutes for individual accounts. Up to several hours for large Organizations with many member accounts.
Can I share outside my org?
No. Managed Entitlements only works within your AWS Organization boundary.
What about existing subscriptions?
Today this supports new subscriptions only (post Oct 30, 2025). Existing subscription support is on the roadmap.
